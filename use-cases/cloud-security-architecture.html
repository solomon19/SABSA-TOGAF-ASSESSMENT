Use Case: Secure Multi-Cloud Retail Platform

A real-world example of applying SABSA and TOGAF 10 to design and operate a resilient, compliant, and scalable cloud architecture.

Context & Drivers (SABSA Contextual / TOGAF Vision)

  • Rapid e-commerce growth across regions; seasonal spikes
  • PII and payment data subject to GDPR and PCI DSS
  • Board mandate for Zero Trust and accelerated delivery

Concepts & Principles

  • Business Attributes: Availability, Integrity, Privacy, Traceability
  • TOGAF principles for reuse, portability, and data ownership
  • Control objectives mapped to NIST CSF and ISO 27001

Logical Architecture Highlights

  • Identity federation (OIDC/SAML) + per-app conditional access
  • Segmentation: Internet → WAF → API Gateway → Services → Data
  • Central secrets, KMS-backed encryption, classification-driven controls
  • Supply chain: SCA, SAST/DAST, signed artifacts, policy gating
